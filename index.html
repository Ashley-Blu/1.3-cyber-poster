Steps to Protect Your Computer from Cyber Threats

Follow these essential tips to keep your data and devices secure.

Use Strong Passwords

Create complex passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Use a password manager to keep track of them.

Enable 2FA

Two-Factor Authentication adds an extra layer of security, requiring a second verification step to log in.

Update Your Software

Install updates for your OS and applications promptly. Updates often include critical security patches.

Beware of Phishing

Be suspicious of unsolicited emails. Don't click on strange links or download unknown attachments.

Use Antivirus Software

Install reputable antivirus and anti-malware software to detect and remove threats automatically.

Back Up Your Data

Regularly back up important files to an external drive or cloud service to recover from a ransomware attack.

Stay Vigilant, Stay Secure!
